About Us

What We Do

Free Voice Generator is a free, no-signup text-to-speech tool that converts any written text into natural-sounding audio in over 50 languages. Whether you need a voiceover for a YouTube video, a podcast, an e-learning course, or just want to hear your writing read aloud, our tool is built for you.

Why We Built This

Most voice generation tools require expensive subscriptions, API keys, or complicated setups. We believe everyone deserves access to high-quality, natural-sounding voices without paying a monthly fee. Our tool is completely free to use, with no account required and no hidden limits.

Our Technology

We use state-of-the-art neural text-to-speech voices that produce natural, human-like audio across dozens of languages including English, Urdu, Hindi, Arabic, Spanish, French, Chinese, and many more. All voice generation happens in real time with no data stored on our servers.

Privacy First

We do not store your text, your audio files, or any personal information. Every voice generation request is processed and immediately discarded. Your content stays yours.
